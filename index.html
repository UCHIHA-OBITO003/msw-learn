🔧 What is a Worker Service?

A worker service is like a helpful robot that does tasks for websites. It's like having a magical assistant that can:

  • 📝 Save your drawings and stories
  • 🎨 Change colors and make things pretty
  • 🔍 Find things you're looking for
  • 🎮 Play games with you
